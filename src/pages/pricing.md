## How credits work

> Credits power scheduled actions.
> Each scheduled start or stop consumes credits.
> Larger or more complex resources use slightly more.

**Typical usage**

- EC2 instance: ~1 credit per action
- RDS database: ~2 credits per action

Credits reset monthly. Need more? Purchase credit packs anytime.

## Overage and add-ons

## Frequently asked questions

### Do credits expire?

Credits reset monthly and do not roll over.

### What happens if I run out of credits?

Schedules keep running. You can add more credits instantly or upgrade your plan.

### Does instance size affect pricing?

Instance size influences credit usage slightly, but you never pay AWS-style per-hour rates.

### Can I switch plans?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time.
